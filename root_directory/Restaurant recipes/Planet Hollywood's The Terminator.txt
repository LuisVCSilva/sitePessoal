Planet Hollywood's The Terminator

½ oz vodka 
½ oz white rum 
½ oz gin 
½ oz Grand Marnier 
 ½ oz Kahlua 
2 oz sweet & sour mix 
1 oz cranberry juice 
1 splash beer 
 


Combine crushed ice with all ingredients, except beer,
 in a tumbler. Shake. Pour a splash of beer on top and 
serve with a straw. 

Makes 1 serving.
