RED LOBSTER CREAMY CAESAR DRESSING

Serving Size  : 12   Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Salads                           Dressings

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                     
     3/4   c            Bottled Italian dressing
