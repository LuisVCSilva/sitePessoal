  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1      Tbls          Garlic Salt

     1/4  Tsp           Thyme
   1      Tsp           Basil
   1      Tbls          Parsley
     1/4  tsp           Celery Salt
   2      Tbls          Salt
   1      Env.          Cup Of Soup Cream Of Chicken Mix
     1/4  C             Cider Vinegar
